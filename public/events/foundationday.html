Foundation Day

IIIT Bangalore celebrate its foundation day every year on 15th September. The yearlong celebration of the college has reached 20 years. The event celebration is always being special with the chief guests and other dignitaries from various fields. Cultural events of variety genres are the main attraction of the event. It is incredible that over 1000 students are studying in IIIT Bangalore. IIITB is bringing about a transformation by educating students to enable them to play a key role in the global IT scenario through a world class education, research, entrepreneurship and innovation. It is an extraordinary example and inspiration by its Founder Director Prof. S. Sadagopan.
